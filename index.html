About us

Might and Chaos is a relatively new gaming community that is working towards it's first year of existence. Unsatisfied with other gaming communities, due to excessive amounts of rules and stringent attitudes or the opposite, where players were allowed to roam the virtual worlds trolling and wreaking unnecessary havok without any oversight or rules whatsoever. Over the years, as we became more hopeless, jumping from one community to the next, we decided to form our own that lies withing the realm of both extremes.

What is to be expected

We are laid back and carefree, but we're not trolls and we don't disrespect other gamers and we aren't afraid to enforce rules when we need to. Generosity and helpfulness to those who need is a priority for us. If someone needs our help in any of the games we participate in, you will find it within Might and Chaos.

Our current mission is to recruit new and veteran gamers to our community. No matter what game you play, or skill level you belong to, you will be welcome here. We currently play Guild Wars 2, League of Legends and Ark: Survival Evolved as a community. Some other games include, but are not limited to Tera, Guild Wars, Skyrim and a few others. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact Jared, Nick, or George, the administrators and founders of Might and Chaos. Use the contact us button at the bottom of the page.
